CM-LIUW-Odometry is a multimodal SLAM framework based on the Iterated Error-State Kalman Filter. It tightly fuses LiDAR-inertial odometry with UWB for global alignment, integrates wheel odometry enhanced by nonholonomic constraints and lever-arm compensation for UWB-outage zones, and employs an adaptive motion-mode switching mechanism based on UWB coverage and environmental degradation.
