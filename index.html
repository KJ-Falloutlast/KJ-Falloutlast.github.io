Chiyun Noh

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at the RPM Robotics lab, Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Ayoung Kim.

My research interests include Radar-Inertial Odometry and Sensor Fusion. I am particularly interested in leveraging the estimated local gravity to refine the rotation matrix.
