**CM-LIUW-Odometry** is a multimodal SLAM system for large-scale, GPS-denied coal mine tunnels. It fuses LiDAR, IMU, UWB, and wheel odometry to improve robustness under feature-poor environments, UWB outage, and wheel odometry uncertainty.
